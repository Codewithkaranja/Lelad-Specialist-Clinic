Cardiac Evaluation

ECG Echocardiography Consultant Review

We assess and manage heart-related conditions including hypertension, heart failure, and cardiovascular disease. Our evaluation combines clinical review with diagnostic testing to guide treatment and monitoring.

Call to Book Visit Clinic

If you have chest pain, sudden shortness of breath, fainting, or severe symptoms, seek urgent care immediately.

Who this service helps

  • High blood pressure (hypertension) assessment and control plans
  • Heart failure reviews (symptoms, treatment response, follow-ups)
  • Palpitations, dizziness, unexplained fatigue, and cardiac risk reviews

What to expect

  • History + physical exam (symptoms, risks, prior meds)
  • Tests as needed (ECG, Echo, labs) for accurate assessment
  • Clear treatment plan and monitoring schedule

Prepare for your visit

  • Bring your medication list (or photos of prescriptions)
  • Carry previous ECG/Echo reports if available
  • Note symptoms: when they occur, triggers, duration

Laboratory Services

Common testing support

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you plan your visit.

Do I need an appointment?

Booking is recommended so you’re seen efficiently. Call ahead to confirm availability and timing.

What should I carry for my consultation?

Bring a medication list, previous test results (if available), and notes about symptoms and duration.

Can you do tests on the same day?

Yes—where applicable, on-site diagnostics and lab testing can support same-day assessment.

Do you support chronic care follow-ups?

Yes. Chronic conditions benefit from monitoring and medication review. We provide structured follow-up guidance.

Do you accept M-Pesa payments?

Yes. Your main site indicates M-Pesa and NFC mobile payments are accepted.

How do I get directions?

Use the “Get Directions” button on the home page contact section, or call the clinic for guidance.
