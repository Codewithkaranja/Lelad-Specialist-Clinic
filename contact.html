FAQs

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended to reduce waiting time, especially for specialist reviews and diagnostics.

Can I come with previous lab results?

Yes — bring previous tests, scans, medication lists, and discharge summaries. It improves accuracy and saves time.

Do you offer ECG and Echo?

Yes. ECG and Echo are available as part of cardiac evaluation when clinically indicated.
