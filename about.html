Our mission

To provide premium, consultant-led internal medicine care that is accurate, compassionate, and practical—helping patients feel better now while preventing future complications.

Whether you are coming in for a new concern or ongoing chronic disease management, we aim to deliver clear explanations, personalised treatment plans, and consistent follow-up.

Thorough Evaluation Accurate Diagnosis Prevention Focus Patient-Centered

What makes us different

We prioritise careful physician assessment and use diagnostics to support decisions—so treatment is targeted, monitoring is structured, and you understand your care plan.

What you can expect

  • Clear consultation process: history, examination, and a plan
  • Diagnostic support where needed (not “tests for tests’ sake”)
  • Medication review and guidance to support adherence
  • Follow-up schedule for chronic conditions

Conditions we commonly support

  • Diabetes and metabolic disorders
  • Hypertension, heart disease, heart failure
  • Kidney disease (acute and chronic)
  • Chest/Asthma clinic support and respiratory concerns
  • General internal medicine reviews and follow-up care
Consultant Physician

Dr. Ngami Mutwa

MBChB, MMed (Internal Medicine)

Dr. Ngami Mutwa is a Consultant Physician with advanced training in Internal Medicine, focused on adult care for both acute and chronic medical conditions. His clinical interests include diabetes care, hypertension, heart disease, kidney health, and complex metabolic disorders—managed through evidence-based, patient-centered care.

At Lelad Specialist Clinic, consultations are structured to ensure clarity: a thorough assessment, appropriate diagnostics when needed, and a plan you can follow—plus follow-up monitoring to support long-term health outcomes.
